Auth Modal — Overlay Style

When Sign In / Sign Up is clicked on the landing page, which overlay treatment feels right?

A — Dark overlay + blur

Background dims to ~65% dark and blurs. Modal card sits centered. Classic modal feel — focused, not jarring.

B — Heavy blur + deep dark

More aggressive blur (background nearly invisible) + 82% dark overlay. Dramatic, immersive feel. Google-style.

C — Blur + bottom sheet

Modal slides up from the bottom like a sheet. Works well on mobile too. Lighter overlay lets the background stay more visible.
