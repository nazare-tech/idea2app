# Research

This folder stores sanitized Maker Compass research artifacts.

## Folders

- `sanitized/`: cleaned summaries from individual research transcripts or notes.
- `processed/`: synthesis across one or more sanitized research artifacts.
- `decisions/`: decisions made because of research.

Raw transcripts do not belong here. If raw transcript files must exist locally, keep them under `.private/raw-transcripts/research/`, which is ignored by git.

Before processing research, read `docs/operating-system/transcript-sanitization-protocol.md` and `docs/operating-system/research-format.md`.
